Your Safety Is Our Priority

Prevent

  • Extra cleaning and sanitary precautions ensure your safety.
  • Enhanced prescreening measures are in place for every client prior to their office on- board.
  • Physical distancing safeguards limit close contact in our workstations, Lobbies, meeting rooms, Pantry and waiting areas.

Protect

  • Masks for visitors and clients are provided for added protection and visitor are limited to reduce gathering.
  • Frequent cleanings on high-touch surfaces such as ad doorknobs, plug points, tabletops, chair handles, handles, sinks and faucets occur to keep you safe.
  • Thorough cleaning and disinfection occur in all workstation areas between client visits.

Support

  • For your comfort and convenience, we offer flexible scheduling options which include systematic appointment process for client's business visitors.
  • We provide timely information, updates and resources to help you make smart meeting decisions.
  • Our team receives ongoing training and guidance to further increase your safety.
